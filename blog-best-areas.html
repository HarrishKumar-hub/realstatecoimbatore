Best Areas to Buy Plots Near Coimbatore in 2025

By Sakthi, LPS Real Estate February 2025 7 min read

Coimbatore's land market has been growing steadily. With IT parks, textile industries, hospitals, and educational institutions expanding, demand for plots has increased significantly. Here is my honest, experience-based assessment of the best areas to buy plots in 2025.

Note: Land prices change frequently. This article reflects general market observations as of early 2025. Contact us for current prices in specific survey numbers.

1. Saravanampatti – Best for Working Professionals

Why to buy here: Saravanampatti is Coimbatore's IT and tech corridor. With multiple tech parks, MNC offices, and good infrastructure, demand for residential plots here has been consistently high.

  • Well-connected to Coimbatore city centre (12-15 km)
  • Good road infrastructure — 4-lane and 6-lane roads
  • Many DTCP approved layouts available
  • Schools, hospitals, malls, restaurants all nearby
  • High rental demand (good for investment buyers)

Honest note: Land prices here are higher than other areas. Best for buyers with a moderate-to-higher budget who want appreciating value.

2. Sulur – Best for Airport Connectivity

Why to buy here: Sulur is close to Coimbatore International Airport and the Coimbatore–Salem NH544 highway. This makes it ideal for frequent travellers, logistics businesses, and investment buyers.

  • Very close to airport (5-7 km)
  • NH544 highway access — great connectivity
  • More affordable than Saravanampatti
  • Growing industrial and warehouse belt nearby
  • Many DTCP approved layouts at competitive prices

Honest note: Some parts of Sulur still lack developed civic amenities. Choose plots near the main road for better connectivity.

3. Annur – Best for Agricultural Land & Farm Living

Why to buy here: Annur is a growing taluk to the north of Coimbatore city with fertile agricultural land at relatively affordable prices. Many NRI buyers and retired families are settling here.

  • Fertile red soil — suitable for coconut, banana, drumstick cultivation
  • Water availability (borewell and canal water in some areas)
  • Quiet, green environment — good for farm houses
  • Lower land prices compared to city areas
  • Improving road connectivity via NH947

Honest note: Monthly market commute into the city takes about 30-40 minutes. Not ideal if you need daily city access.

4. Singanallur – Best for Established City Living

Why to buy here: Singanallur is an established residential area within city limits with all civic amenities fully developed. It's suitable for buyers who want to be in the heart of the city while still getting a plot.

  • Within Coimbatore city corporation limits
  • Fully developed — water, sewage, electricity, roads all in place
  • Very close to Coimbatore bus stand, railway station
  • Schools, hospitals, markets within walking distance

Honest note: Limited availability of large plots. Most available plots are 2-4 cents. Higher prices due to developed location.

5. Perur & Kinathukadavu – Best Budget Option

Why to buy here: For buyers with a limited budget who still want residential or agricultural land near Coimbatore, Perur and Kinathukadavu offer the best value. Land prices here are among the lowest of the Coimbatore district.

  • Very affordable land prices (30-40% lower than city areas)
  • Peaceful residential environment
  • Improving road connectivity
  • Good for long-term investment (5–10 year horizon)
